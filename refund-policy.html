Refund Policy

Refund review for direct-access testing orders.

This Refund Policy explains how eligible refund requests may be reviewed for Axis Point Premium™ direct-access testing workflows and related collection options.

Draft notice: This page is provided as practical refund policy language for your prototype. It should be reviewed by qualified legal counsel before production use.

Effective date: June 2026

This Refund Policy applies to eligible Axis Point Premium™ direct-access testing orders and related collection workflows. Refund eligibility depends on the order status, collection status, kit shipment status, payment processing, laboratory accessioning, and any costs already incurred.

1. General Policy

AccessPoint Dx and Axis Point Premium™ may allow refund requests for eligible direct-access testing orders before certain processing steps have begun. Refund eligibility depends on order status, collection status, kit shipment status, payment processing, and laboratory accessioning.

2. Eligible Refund Situations

A refund may be considered when an order has been placed but has not yet been collected, shipped, accessioned, or processed by the laboratory. Refund requests should be submitted as soon as possible after purchase.

3. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds may not be available once a collection appointment has been completed, an at-home kit has shipped, a specimen has been collected, a specimen has been accessioned, testing has begun, results have been released, or third-party processing costs have already been incurred.

4. Missed Appointments and Rescheduling

Missed appointments may be eligible for rescheduling depending on operational availability. Repeated missed appointments, late cancellations, or inability to complete collection may affect refund eligibility.

5. At-Home Collection Kits

At-home collection kits may become non-refundable after shipment. If a kit is damaged, lost, or unusable, AccessPoint Dx may review the situation and determine whether replacement, reshipment, credit, or another resolution is appropriate.

6. Testing Limitations

Laboratory testing may occasionally be delayed, rejected, or unable to be completed because of specimen quality, insufficient sample, stability limits, collection error, identification mismatch, shipping delay, or other operational issues. Resolution may include recollection, replacement, credit, or refund depending on the circumstances.

7. Payment Processing Fees

Certain payment processing fees, third-party fees, shipping costs, collection fees, or administrative costs may be non-refundable where permitted by law and disclosed during checkout.

8. How to Request a Refund

To request review of a refund, contact AccessPoint Dx with your name, order reference number, date of purchase, and reason for the request.

9. Review Timeline

Refund requests are reviewed based on order status and operational records. Approved refunds may take several business days to appear depending on the payment method and financial institution.

10. Changes to This Policy

AccessPoint Dx may update this Refund Policy periodically. Updated policy language will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
