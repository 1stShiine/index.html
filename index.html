Our Clinical Philosophy

Diagnostics should connect people to care — not bypass it.

AccessPoint Dx is built as a local diagnostic infrastructure partner. Provider relationships remain the foundation of the platform. Direct-access testing exists where appropriate for self-pay wellness and preventive testing, with clear boundaries around diagnosis, treatment, and medical decision-making.

Provider-first

The site presents AccessPoint Dx as a partner to local care organizations, not a replacement.

Clear boundaries

Consumer testing is positioned for wellness awareness and appropriate follow-up.

Local continuity

Reports are designed to be easier to share with local providers and care teams.

Why Local Processing Matters

Closer diagnostic pathways can support faster answers and better continuity.

National testing options already exist. AccessPoint Dx differentiates by building a Minnesota-centered model: provider partnerships first, consumer access where appropriate, and occupational screening where required — all through separated pathways that protect trust.
